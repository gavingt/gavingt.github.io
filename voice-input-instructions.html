Voice input can be used to enter a variety of data, as shown below:

To enter a tip amount, say the amount as a decimal number or as a price. For example, if the tip is $5.24, you can say "five dollars and twenty-four cents or "five point two four".

To enter a delivery address, simply say the first line of the address. For example, if the full address is 1600 Pennsylvania Ave, Washington, DC 20006, you can just say "1600 Pennsylvania Avenue". The app will then search for the most likely nearby address that matches what you said.

To enter a delivery address and automatically start navigation, say "navigate to" followed by the address. For example, if the full address is 1600 Pennsylvania Avenue, say "navigate to 1600 Pennsylvania Avenue".

To enter a phone number, say each of the numbers individually. For example, if the phone number is (555) 498-0719, say "five five five four nine eight zero seven one nine".

To enter an order price, say the phrase "price equals" followed by the amount. For example, if the order price is $16.70, say "price equals 16.7".

To enter a prepaid tip amount, say the phrase "tip amount equals" followed by the amount. For example, if the prepaid tip amount is $5.20, say "tip amount equals 5.2".

For upcoming orders, each order gets its own 'Voice input' button (outlined in red above).
